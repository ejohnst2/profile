Favorite Books

The Alchemist

The Alchemist is a novel by Brazilian author Paulo Coelho which was first published in 1988. Originally written in Portuguese, it became an international bestseller translated into some 70 languages as of 2016. An allegorical novel, The Alchemist follows a young Andalusian shepherd in his journey to Egypt, after having a recurring dream of finding treasure there.

Elon Musk

Elon Musk is the most daring entrepreneur of our time. Few names in our history are matched by his ingenious vision and tireless dynamism. A mix of Thomas Edison, Henry Ford, Howard Hughes and Steve Jobs, Musk is the man behind PayPal, Tesla Motors, SpaceX and SolarCity, all companies responsible for true revolutions in the American industry and corporate environment.

Guns, Germs and Steel

Guns, Germs, and Steel: The Fates of Human Societies is a book by Jared Diamond, professor of geography at UCLA. In 1998, the work received the Pulitzer Prize and the Aventis Award for best scientific book.
